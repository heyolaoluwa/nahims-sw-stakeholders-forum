Featured Initiatives

Highlights of Our Projects

Our flagship programmes are designed to create lasting impact across communities and institutions throughout Southwest Nigeria.

HIM Capacity Building

A structured capacity development programme training health information managers across Southwest facilities to improve data quality, coding accuracy, and patient record management standards.

Digital Health Records Drive

Supporting the transition from paper-based to electronic health records in public health facilities, equipping HIM professionals with the digital skills needed for modern healthcare delivery.

Health Data Advocacy

Partnering with state ministries of health and NHIA to advocate for proper health data governance, privacy standards, and the strategic role of HIM professionals in the healthcare system.

Research & Publication Hub

Facilitating peer-reviewed research among NAHIMS SW members, connecting professionals with academic institutions for joint publications and conference presentations in health informatics.
